Our Absolute Commitment: We do not collect, upload, transmit, or share any of your personal privacy data.

  • We do NOT actively request or collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as names, phone numbers, contact lists, or geographical location data.

  • We do NOT implement any background silent listening/monitoring modules. The application is guaranteed to provide full and premium clock services even in a completely offline environment.

02. App Permissions & Technical Necessity

In order to correctly render high-quality clock animations and provide a fluid, seamless user experience on the Android operating system, the application may rely on the following low-level system mechanical capabilities:

LOCAL STATE PERSISTENCE

Local Configuration Storage

This app utilizes standard Android SharedPreferences and sandbox mechanisms to locally persist your personalized clock preferences, such as the 12/24-hour mode setting and the chosen ambient glow theme index. This configuration data remains 100% on your local device and is never uploaded to any cloud server.

UI REFRESH & TICK ALIGNMENT

System Clock Refreshing

The app only uses standard, non-sensitive hardware refresh mechanisms to trigger accurate second-by-second time updates and smooth visual transitions (Canvas drawing). This app does NOT request or contain sensitive permissions such as access to SMS, camera, contacts, or photo gallery.
